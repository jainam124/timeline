The Human Pangenome Project begins
>Text:
The Human Genome Project was a vital advancement in genomics, but it did not consist of a diverse human population. The Human Pangenome Project promises to diversify our knowledge of human DNA sequences and to collect whole-genome sequences from diverse populations around the world.
