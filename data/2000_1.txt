>Headline:
US President Clinton and UK Prime Minister Blair support free access to genome information
>Text:
Together, the President of the United States and the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom agreed to grant the public free access to genomic information from the Human Genome Project. The information was now at the disposal of the public and was subject to two conditions. The data was to be distributed as much as possible, and genetic technology had to be open to private investigation.
