>Headline:
US President Clinton and UK Prime Minister Blair support free access to genome information
>Text:
Together, the President of the United States and the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom agreed to grant the public free access to genome information that scientists all over the world had contributed to. The information at the disposal of the public was subject to two main ideals. Firstly, data was to be shared as widely as possible. Secondly, genetic technology was to be open to private investigation.
